Hack: Air Dry Clothing

Air drying your clothing instead of using a dryer is great for the environment, and for you! It helps you to save energy and curb emissions. For many homes, the dryer is the appliance that uses the third most energy (after the fridge and washer). By air-drying your clothing, it can reduce the carbon footprint of an average household by 2,400 lbs per year! On average, households spend more than $100(USD)/year on the electricity used for the dryer. This means that your purchase of a rack to hang your clothing should be paid back within the year you got it. You can also increase the life of your clothing by air drying. The lint found in our dryers is our clothing wearing down. Also, it reduces wrinkles in the clothing, without the need for ironing.
