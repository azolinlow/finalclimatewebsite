Hack: Switch to LED light bulbs

Standard LED bulbs can be up to 80% more energy efficient than regular light bulbs. They also tend to waste way less energy than other types of lighting. One of the more inefficient lights, fluorescent lights, only convert around 5% of the energy they produce into light (meaning that 95% becomes heat energy). Many commercial buildings use fluorescent tube lights, despite their inefficiency. Fluorescent lights are also toxic for the environment, because they contain toxic chemicals and elements, including mercury. Unfortunately, these chemicals leak into the environment once they are disposed of in landfills. Good quality LEDs are able to last more than 25 times longer than traditional light bulbs, and are far more efficient.
