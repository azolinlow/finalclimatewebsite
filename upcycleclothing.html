Hack: Upcycle Clothing

This hack comes back to reducing fashion waste, something that causes some serious environmental issues (see the page on fashion here). By choosing to upcycle clothing, you save it from going into the landfill. Another upside of upcycling your clothing, especially if you haven't sewn before, is learning a new, very useful skill. Upcycling means taking something and making it more valuble, unlike recycling, which is just changing one item into another.
