Hack: Use less water

Water efficient shower heads are pretty cheap, as well as being easy to install without help from a plumber. Not only does it help the environment, it means you can keep showering for the same amount of time without using as much water. This will also help you save money on your water bill. High efficiency shower heads use 5.6-7.6L of water/min or less, according to the CRD's official website. The shower head linked below uses only 6.6 L of water per minute.
